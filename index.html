Professional Summary

ELA educator and instructional technologist with over 25 years of experience across secondary and post-secondary settings, including more than a decade in large urban districts serving Title I and ESL-majority populations. My work integrates curriculum design, instructional technology, and clear, assessment-aligned materials that support diverse learners.

I design TEKS-aligned lessons, Demonstrations of Learning (DOLs), differentiated tasks, and scaffolded reading/writing materials, while contributing to campus-wide efforts to improve student outcomes. My instructional technology background includes LMS development, online learning design, SCORM content, and work on CLEO, Texas A&M’s open-source synchronous and asynchronous tutoring platform. I stay current with emerging AI tools—including LLMs, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), local models, and agentic workflows—and understand the importance of using these responsibly within FERPA-aligned educational environments.
